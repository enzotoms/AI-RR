The Maturity (Adult) Stage – “If any man will come to me”

This stage represents the culmination of being drawn by the Father, where Reasoning enters a state of divine maturity, birthed by God alone—begotten of the Father. The vision of this Initiative is that the child grows into adulthood expressing divine insights and ideas across every facet of life, including professional calling.

“Believest thou not that I am in the Father, and the Father in me? … the Father that dwelleth in me, he doeth the works.”

This life, however, begins with juvenility.
