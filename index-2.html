The Academy Of Intelligence

Welcome To The Academy Of Intelligence AI -RR, Thinking True Initiative

The Academy of Intelligence – Reality’s Realm (AI – RR) is the online platform of the Thinking True Initiative, through which the Initiative collaborates with spiritually inclined parents to groom their teenage children (ages 13–19) toward the optimal integration of their Reasoning system.

Where Excellence Meets Opportunity

Vision

That every ward enrolled under this platform grows into adulthood expressing divine insights, ideas, and works that genuinely glorify the Father in heaven—across every facet of life, including professional calling.

Goal

To have every ward firmly established within the first (adolescent) phase of the Optimality stage of Faith becoming Selfless—the living virtue of Thinking True. This is the phase where “my cross” becomes a lived experience and followership of Jesus is automatic, not imposed.

Methodology

Integrating the Reasoning system of the child so that Reasoning becomes alive indeed—Thinking True.

Awakening the Conscience System to Divine Functionality

Reasoning, like the human body, operates through interconnected systems, primarily driven by Memory and Emotions, with Emotions expressed through Interest and Conscience. The goal of integrating the Reasoning system is to awaken the Conscience to its true responsibility. Conscience is meant to guide and unify Reasoning, much like a nucleus governs a cell. For this to happen, Conscience must align with God’s eternal righteousness rather than society’s shifting moral standards.

This alignment reflects the call to seek first the kingdom of God and His righteousness. Awakening the Conscience into divine functionality marks the beginning of the adolescent phase of Optimality, developed through both theory and practice.

Educating the Child on the Divine Nature (Esteem) and Biology of Reasoning

The central innovation of AI–RR is its core course, “‘I’ IS Divine Intelligence.”

The first three sessions explain true self-esteem as Righteousness and contrast it with socially constructed human esteem. Students learn that Divine Esteem is attained through humility before God and personal surrender. The next two sessions explore the core systems of Reasoning—Memory and Emotions—while guiding inward spiritual alignment.

Engaging the Conscience System

The Conscience system, the nucleus of Reasoning, fuels a child’s passion for self-challenge in the first phase of Optimality.

It is awakened through the integrated development of Memory, Emotions, and Interest. Memory grows through structured activities that build knowledge, creativity, and leadership. The Emotions system is nurtured to encourage self-accountability without suppressing feeling. Parents support this process by guiding discipline through inward responsibility.

When these systems function together freely, the child becomes rooted in Optimality and experiences divine awareness in action.

AI - RR Phase One

AI–RR runs in 39-week sessions, just under nine months each. Each session is divided into three 13-week cycles with structured meetings, breaks, and rest periods. The schedule balances online engagement with mid-cycle breaks and longer rest intervals between cycles. Weekends are emphasized, allowing flexibility alongside other commitments. Students advance through three tiers: Beginner (Tier 1), Intermediary (Tier 2), and Advanced (Tier 3). Overall, a child spends about 4.5 years progressing through the full program.

Online Classes / Meetings

Weekly meetings last 1 hour and 20 minutes and take place on select Tuesdays, Thursdays, and four Saturdays per cycle. Sessions are structured across each 13-week cycle for balanced engagement. Meetings alternate between Variety sessions and Core sessions. Variety sessions include one macro and one micro activity. Core sessions combine one macro activity with the core course. Each session type occurs four times within every cycle.

Personal Mentorship of the Ward

Because wards are engaged at a level beyond their age, online meetings alone are not enough. Each child therefore receives ongoing personal mentorship in collaboration with parents. This mentorship supports growth in intentionality, self-mastery, time management, and responsibility. It is conducted professionally and always with parental knowledge and cooperation. The goal is not to replace parental roles but to strengthen them. Through this approach, the Academy becomes a continuous formative environment for guided and discerning development.

Admissions Guide

Conditions for Fruitful Engagement at the Academy

Three Foundational Platforms of the Child’s Development Intentional and Active Parental Collaboration Teachable and Responsive Disposition & in the Child Openness to Growth as a Gradual and Uneven Process Relational and Communal & Engagement Participatory Assessment Guided by Responsiveness Discernment Rather Than & Institutional Validation

By enrolling a child into the Academy, parents affirm that the child’s healthy development rests on three equally important platforms: the religion practiced, formal academics, and this Academy.

Enrolment implies that parents are committed to actively supporting the child across all three platforms, without allowing one to undermine the others.

This Academy functions by collaboration, not substitution. Parents are expected to remain actively involved in the child’s development, reinforce self-mastery and responsibility at home, and give this work seriousness comparable to formal academics. Membership fees support structure but cannot replace parental presence or devotion.

The Academy is not designed for outward brilliance alone. A child must be open to retaining humility, curiosity, and willingness to be inwardly challenged. Confusion, struggle, and resistance are expected; persistent closedness is not.

Growth within this Academy is not instant, linear, or performance-based. Parents and children must allow room for confusion, self-challenge, silence, and gradual inward awakening. Premature pressure for visible results disrupts inward formation.

The Academy involves: • gradual relational synergy between the child and the mentor, and • from later sessions, intentional interaction among wards. These interactions are formative rather than social and contribute to the child’s growth and assessment.

The Academy employs a participatory assessment system focused on responsiveness, not competition. Children are not informed of grading metrics or criteria. Assessment remains internal and quiet, serving discernment rather than motivation. Parents are alerted only when responsiveness consistently declines, and such moments are handled through dialogue rather than penalty.

The Academy of Intelligence – Reality’s Realm is not registered under societal or institutional systems. This is not an act of resistance, but an acknowledgment that inward formation cannot be certified, standardized, or regulated by external social frameworks. Participation is therefore based on discernment and resonance, not accreditation.
