Welcome To The Academy Of Intelligence (AI -RR)

The Academy of Intelligence – Reality’s Realm (AI – RR) is the online platform of the Thinking True Initiative, through which the Initiative collaborates with spiritually inclined parents to groom their teenage children (ages 13–19) toward the optimal integration of their Reasoning system.

Vision

That every ward enrolled under this platform grows into adulthood expressing divine insights, ideas, and works that genuinely glorify the Father in heaven—across every facet of life, including professional calling.

Goal

To have every ward firmly established within the first (adolescent) phase of the Optimality stage of Faith becoming Selfless—the living virtue of Thinking True. This is the phase where “my cross” becomes a lived experience and followership of Jesus is automatic, not imposed.

Methodology

Integrating the Reasoning system of the child so that Reasoning becomes alive indeed—Thinking True.

Binding Principle of the Academy

“‘I’ IS not as simple as ‘me.’”

“In fact, nothing is that simple except the self-sufficient Human Mind (‘me’), which cannot help but think things simple. Therefore, ‘I’ must keep unfolding—the most distinct creation by Original Righteousness/Intelligence, God, the mysterious Father.”

Humanly oriented Reasoning:

who “I am” – “Me”

Divinely Inclined Reasoning:

Integrating the Reasoning System:
Awakening the Conscience System to Divine Functionality

Like the physical body, Reasoning is comprised of different systems. These systems are driven by two core faculties: Memory and Emotions, with the Emotions faculty further expressed through the Interest and Conscience systems.

Integrating the Reasoning system—and engaging the child in the living virtue that Selfless Faith is—has one central aim: bringing the Conscience system alive to its responsibility.

The responsibility of the Conscience system is to integrate Reasoning in the same way the nucleus integrates every cell. For this to occur, Conscience must come alive to the eternal righteousness and capacity of God.

“For I say unto you, That except your righteousness shall exceed the righteousness of the scribes and Pharisees, ye shall in no case enter into the kingdom of heaven.”

Awakening the Conscience system into divine functionality—the first (adolescent) phase of Optimality—involves both theoretical and practical approaches.

Theoritical Approach

Educating the Child on the Divine Nature (Esteem) and Biology of Reasoning

The pivotal innovation of the AI–RR is its core course titled: “‘I’ IS Divine Intelligence.”

The first three sessions of this course provide a methodological exposition of the true basis of self-esteem as Righteousness, and clearly distinguish between Human Esteem (rooted in social righteousness) and Divine Esteem (rooted in God’s eternal righteousness). The next two sessions gradually unveil the structure and function of the core systems of Reasoning—the Memory and Emotions systems—ensuring that the child’s spirit is attuned inwardly as the practical approaches run concurrently.

The final session serves as a point of integration, where all that has been learned is reviewed and consolidated, and the child presents a final project that reflects this integration. Although this course carries depth and complexity, it is well suited to the humility and patience of the developing mind. Its goal is not understanding, but experience—Thinking True / Selfless Faith.

The Three Practical and Concurrent Approaches

This approach concerns the practical engagement of the Conscience system through the stages of Intentionality and Optimality. It involves grooming the child through the following interrelated aspects:

1
Experiencing Memory as a Living System The following activities, which strengthen the experience of Memory as the nutritive agent of Reasoning—supporting curiosity, analytical capacity, and imaginative reach—form the core of our online engagements:
Macro activities (nutrients):
  • Technical exposition (knowledge across the Physical Sciences, Social Sciences, and the Humanities)
  • Cultural Hub (Reasoning identifying with roots)
  • Talent Exhibition
 One macro activity occurs during every weekly meeting.
Micro activities (nutrients):
  • Chess
  • Biography studies and discussions
  • Art appreciation and literature
  • Memory and physical exercises
One micro activity is emphasized in four of the eight meetings per cycle, with light icebreakers present throughout all meetings. Each activity places the child in a guided leadership role, headlining themes and discussions.

(2) Ensuring the Emotions System (Feelings Faculty) Is Alive

This branch ensures that the child is deeply immersed in the capacity to feel, because feelings function as the respiration of Reasoning . Weak respiration results in weak Reasoning posture. While a fuller understanding of the Emotions system is provided later in the core course, parental collaboration is especially critical here. Parents are expected to discipline the child toward self-mastery—not by dissociating the child from emotion, but by guiding emotional expression through inward responsibility.

(3) Establishing the Interest System as the Child’s Crucial Drive

This is achieved by motivating the child toward excellence in natural talents—whether in music, art, sports, or other constructive domains. For this reason, Talent Exhibition is included as a macro activity, allowing the child to express excellence through the Academy’s social channels.

When these three branches operate concurrently and independently of external pressure, the child is firmly established in the adolescent (first) phase of Optimality. Divine awareness follows naturally: “With God, nothing is impossible” becomes lived experience—not toward societal markers of success, but toward insights that abound in the heavenly places.

WHY GIVE THIS SERIOUS CONSIDERATION?

This era, marked by the advent of Artificial Intelligence, belongs to those who can discover the limits of what society compels them to take seriously—career, morality, achievement, and status—and therefore learn to take themselves seriously from within. It is by encountering these limits that Reasoning begins to look beyond external validation and become inwardly responsible for its own life.

In this process, one is not a product of world systems, but inwardly begotten of the Father.

(Jeremiah 31:33; Romans 8:19)
